# Privacy Policy for No Nonsense Meditation

**Last Updated: January 10, 2026**

## The Short Version

**We don't collect any of your data. Period.**

All your meditation data stays on your device. No tracking, no analytics, no servers, no ads.

## What Data Stays on Your Device

The app stores locally on your device:
- Meditation sessions (date, time, duration)
- Your preferences (sounds, settings, reminders)
- Statistics (streak, total time, session count)

## Apple Services (Your Choice)

### iCloud Sync (Optional)
- Syncs your sessions across your devices using your personal iCloud
- You control it in Settings
- We never see your data—it's encrypted in your iCloud

### Apple Health (Optional)
- Writes mindful minutes to Apple Health after sessions
- Requires your permission
- Data goes directly to Apple Health, not through us

### Notifications (Optional)
- Daily reminders are scheduled locally on your device
- No tracking of whether you open them

### Siri & Shortcuts
- Lets you start sessions with voice commands
- Processed by Apple, not us

## What We Don't Do

- No data collection
- No analytics or crash reporting
- No advertising
- No third-party services
- No user tracking
- No servers we control

## Your Data Control

- **Export**: Download your sessions as JSON
- **Import**: Restore from backups
- **Delete**: Clear all data anytime in Settings
